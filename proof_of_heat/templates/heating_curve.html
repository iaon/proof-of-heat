Edit heating curve parameters and preview the resulting supply temperature curve.

Preview

Preview uses target_room_temp_c from heating_mode.params and plots Ft = S * (Tt - Ct)^exponent + O + Tt, where O is the optional offset from heating_curve.offset. The result is clamped between min and max supply temperatures, and the chart is drawn only for outdoor temperatures up to the room target so the exponent stays defined.
